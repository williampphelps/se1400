Competition

Who is my competition?

I would say that my competition is other Full-Stack Developers in the Southern Utah area.

What is the competition doing right?

The better portfolios that I have seen are minimilistic and have good design. They are easy to navigate and have images that showcase their work.

What is the competition doing wrong?

Some of the portfolios I have looked at are difficult to navigate. The flashiness of the different effects take precedence over their content making it difficult to discern what they have done. A lot of portfolios lack images of their design work.

How can I do it better?

I can make sure that I have clear navigation that is intuitive. I can include screenshots/images/videos of my projects. I can make sure that the fun effects aren't too overwhelming.
